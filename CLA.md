# Goblins OS — Individual Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

> **Template — not yet legal advice.** This CLA is a starting point. Have an IP
> attorney review and adapt it before you accept your first outside contribution.
> It is written to keep the Goblins OS project owner able to **dual-license** the
> project (offer commercial/proprietary terms alongside the AGPL) and to keep the
> aggregate copyright **clean and sellable** — the two things that preserve the
> project's acquisition value.

Thank you for contributing to Goblins OS. To protect you, the project, and the
project's owner, we ask every contributor to agree to the terms below. Signing is
typically done by a bot when you open your first pull request, or by adding your
name to `CONTRIBUTORS` and referencing this file in your PR.

In this Agreement, **"Owner"** means the owner of the Goblins OS project (and its
successors and assigns — including an acquirer of the project); **"You"** means the
copyright owner or legal entity authorized to enter this Agreement;
**"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship you submit to the project.

1. **Copyright license.** You grant the Owner and recipients of software distributed
   by the Owner a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable
   copyright license to reproduce, prepare derivative works of, publicly display,
   publicly perform, sublicense, and distribute your Contributions and such
   derivative works.

2. **Right to re-license (dual-licensing).** You agree that the Owner **may license
   your Contributions under any license terms it chooses**, including the project's
   open-source license (AGPL-3.0-or-later) *and* separate commercial or proprietary
   licenses. This lets the Owner offer commercial terms to customers who cannot use
   the AGPL, and lets an acquirer continue to do so. You retain your own copyright;
   this is a license, not an assignment.

3. **Patent license.** You grant the Owner and recipients a perpetual, worldwide,
   non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable (except as below) patent license to make,
   use, sell, offer to sell, import, and otherwise transfer your Contributions,
   covering only patent claims you can license that are necessarily infringed by your
   Contribution alone or in combination with the project. If any entity brings patent
   litigation alleging the project or a Contribution infringes a patent, the patent
   licenses you granted under this Agreement to that entity terminate.

4. **Your representations.** You represent that: (a) each Contribution is your
   original creation, or you have the right to submit it under these terms; (b) if
   your employer has rights to intellectual property you create, you have permission
   to make the Contribution on your employer's behalf, or your employer has waived
   such rights; and (c) you are legally entitled to grant the above licenses.

5. **No obligation.** You are not expected to provide support for your Contributions,
   except to the extent you wish. Contributions are provided "as is" without
   warranties of any kind, to the extent permitted by law.

6. **Notice of inaccuracy.** You agree to notify the Owner if any statement above
   becomes inaccurate.

---

By submitting a Contribution, you agree to the terms of this Agreement.

_Consider, with counsel, whether a copyright **assignment** (rather than this license
grant) better fits your acquisition goals — assignment gives an acquirer the cleanest
title, while a license keeps contributors as copyright holders. Many projects choose
this license-plus-relicensing model as the contributor-friendly middle ground._
